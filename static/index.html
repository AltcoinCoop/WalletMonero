Why monero?

Monero is a decentralized, secure and anonymous cryptocurrency. With Monero only you control and are responsible for your means. All your transactions and accounts are kept out of the reach.

DECENTRALIZED
Monero is a decentralized cryptocurrency managed by a network of users, and each transaction is confirmed by them.
SECURE
Monero uses ring signatures to hide personal data, which is achieved by using one-time keys for each individual transaction.
ANONYMOUS
Monero mixes transactions, addresses and amounts, so transactions on a blockchain can’t be associated with specific users.
